Webull Trade Entry Helper

Since CSV Export Isn't Available:

  1. Open your Webull app and go to your trade history
  2. For each trade, fill in the form below with the details
  3. Click "Generate Trade JSON" to create the trade data
  4. Copy the JSON output
  5. Use the "Quick Add" feature in your journal or manually add via API

Trade Entry Form

Tip: You can also take a screenshot of your Webull trade history and manually enter the trades. This helper makes it faster by generating the correct format for you.
