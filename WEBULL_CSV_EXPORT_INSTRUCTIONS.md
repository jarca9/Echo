# How to Download CSV from Webull

## Important: Webull sends CSV via Email

**Note:** Webull doesn't provide direct download. Instead, they email you the CSV file. The process takes 5-10 minutes.

## Method 1: Webull Mobile App (Easiest)

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Open Webull App**
   - Launch the Webull mobile app on your phone
   - Log in to your account

2. **Go to Account**
   - Tap the **Webull logo** at the bottom center of the screen
   - Or tap the "Account" tab at the bottom

3. **Navigate to History**
   - Tap on the **"History"** tab
   - You should see your order/trade history

4. **Export Orders**
   - Look for the **"Export Orders"** icon (download symbol ⬇️) at the **top right corner**
   - Tap it

5. **Confirm Email**
   - Enter or confirm your email address
   - Tap **"Submit"**

6. **Check Your Email**
   - Wait 5-10 minutes
   - Check your email inbox (and spam folder)
   - Webull will send you a CSV file attachment
   - Download the CSV file from the email

## Method 2: Webull Desktop App

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Open Webull Desktop**
   - Launch the Webull desktop application
   - Log in to your account

2. **Go to Account Section**
   - Navigate to the **"Account"** section
   - Look for the **"Orders"** widget/panel

3. **Export Orders**
   - In the Orders widget, click the **Settings icon** (three horizontal lines ☰) at the **top right corner**
   - Select **"Export Orders"** from the menu

4. **Confirm Email**
   - Enter or confirm your email address
   - Click **"OK"**

5. **Check Your Email**
   - Wait 5-10 minutes
   - Check your email inbox
   - Download the CSV file from the email attachment

## Method 3: Webull Website

1. **Log in to Webull Website**
   - Go to: https://www.webull.com
   - Sign in to your account

2. **Navigate to Account/Orders**
   - Click on your account/profile
   - Go to "Orders" or "Order History"

3. **Export Orders**
   - Look for export/download option
   - Follow similar steps as desktop app
   - Confirm email and wait for CSV via email

## What the CSV Contains:

The exported CSV file includes:
- All your orders (filled, partially filled, pending, working, canceled, failed)
- Trade dates and times
- Symbols/tickers
- Buy/Sell actions
- Quantities
- Prices
- Commissions/fees
- Order status

## Quick Summary:

**Mobile App:**
1. Tap Webull logo → Account → History tab
2. Tap Export Orders icon (⬇️) at top right
3. Confirm email → Submit
4. Wait 5-10 minutes → Check email → Download CSV

**Desktop App:**
1. Account → Orders widget
2. Settings icon (☰) → Export Orders
3. Confirm email → OK
4. Wait 5-10 minutes → Check email → Download CSV

## If You Can't Find Export Option:

1. **Check Different Sections:**
   - Try "Order History" instead of "Trade History"
   - Look in "Account Settings" or "Reports"
   - Check "Statements" section

2. **Contact Webull Support:**
   - They can guide you to the exact location
   - Or provide alternative export methods

3. **Use Screenshot/Manual Entry:**
   - As a last resort, take screenshots
   - Or manually enter trades (the journal supports quick entry)

## CSV Format Expected:

The CSV should contain columns like:
- Date/Time
- Symbol/Ticker
- Action (Buy/Sell)
- Quantity/Shares
- Price
- Commission/Fee
- Order Type
- Status

## After Receiving the Email:

1. **Open the email from Webull**
   - Subject line usually says something like "Your Order History" or "Trade Export"
   - Check spam folder if you don't see it

2. **Download the CSV attachment**
   - Click the CSV file attachment
   - Save it to your Desktop or Downloads folder
   - File name is usually something like: `webull_orders_YYYYMMDD.csv`

3. **Import into Echo Trading Journal**
   - Open your Echo trading journal (http://localhost:5001)
   - Click **"Import CSV"** in the sidebar (under CSV section)
   - Select the downloaded CSV file
   - Trades will be automatically imported and parsed!

## Pro Tips:

- **Set up email forwarding**: Forward Webull export emails to a specific folder for easy access
- **Export regularly**: Export weekly or monthly to keep your journal updated
- **Check spam folder**: Webull emails sometimes go to spam
- **Multiple accounts**: If you have multiple Webull accounts, export from each separately

## Tips:

- **Export regularly**: Export CSV daily or weekly to keep your journal up to date
- **Date range**: Export all trades for complete history, or just recent trades for updates
- **File naming**: Rename files with dates (e.g., `webull_2024-12-08.csv`) for easy tracking
- **Backup**: Keep exported CSVs as backup records

## Troubleshooting:

**"Export button not found":**
- Try different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Check if you're on the correct account page
- Look for "Download" or "Share" options

**"CSV won't import":**
- Check file format (should be .csv)
- Verify CSV has required columns
- Try opening CSV in Excel first to verify format
- Contact support if format seems incorrect

**"Missing trades in CSV":**
- Check date range in export settings
- Verify all trades are included in the selected period
- Export multiple times if needed for different date ranges
